Information



Rubbing is an impression of a design on brass or stone, made by rubbing on paper laid over it with colored wax, pencil, chalk, etc. For this project, I've used crayon on US Letter sized paper. There are partial rubbings indicating the impressions could not fit all the information of US Letter sized paper. There are full- rubbings indicating all of the impressions are rubbed on the US letter sized paper.
